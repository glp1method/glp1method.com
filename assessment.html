What is food noise? The persistent mental chatter about food that lives in your head regardless of whether you're physically hungry — the constant planning, the pull toward certain foods, the cravings that show up even right after eating. GLP-1 medications quiet it for many people. But quieter isn't the same as resolved. This assessment helps you understand where you actually are.
